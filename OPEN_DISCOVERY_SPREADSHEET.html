If the spreadsheet does not open automatically:

  1. In Cursor, open the Ports panel and forward port 8765 (or confirm it is already forwarded).
  2. Open http://127.0.0.1:8765/DISCOVERY_SPREADSHEET.html
  3. Or use Command Palette → Simple Browser: Show and paste that URL.
